Welcome to our coffee haven! At Star Hortons we believe that every cup tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Toronto, our cozy and inviting space is the perfect place to unwind, catch up with friends, or simply savor the moment. We pride ourselves on sourcing the finest beans from around the world, roasting them to perfection, and brewing each cup with love and precision. Whether you're a coffee aficionado or just need a quick pick-me-up, our baristas are here to craft the perfect beverage for you.

From rich, bold espressos to creamy lattes and refreshing cold brews, we offer a wide range of options to suit every palate. And it's not just about the coffee – our selection of teas, pastries, and light bites are designed to complement your drink and enhance your experience. Step inside, breathe in the aroma, and let us make your day a little brighter. Welcome to Star Hortons – where every cup is a new beginning.
